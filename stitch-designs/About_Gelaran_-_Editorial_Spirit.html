The Genesis

In the heart of Central Java, where the Solo River whispers tales of ancient kings, Gelaran was born. We recognized a widening gap between the venerable traditions of Surakarta and the rapid pulse of the digital age.

What began as a simple archival project evolved into a sophisticated bridge. We believe that technology should not replace tradition, but rather provide the stage upon which it can perform for a global audience.

Today, Gelaran serves as the definitive curator for cultural events, connecting local artisans, performers, and historians with a new generation of digital natives who seek depth, authenticity, and historical grounding.

Our Mission

To empower cultural practitioners through technological advocacy.

We provide the tools and visibility needed for local curators to sustain their craft in a modern economy without compromising their heritage.

Our Vision

Surakarta as a global beacon of living, breathing heritage.

A future where the artistic soul of Solo is accessible, celebrated, and thriving across every digital and physical frontier.
