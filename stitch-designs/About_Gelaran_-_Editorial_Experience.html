Preserving the Soul of Solo

Gelaran is a digital bridge connecting the timeless artistry of Surakarta with a global audience, ensuring our traditions thrive in the modern age.

The Story Behind
The Curtain

Born in the heart of the Keraton, Gelaran emerged from a simple observation: the vibrant cultural life of Solo was often hidden from the digital world. We believe that a Wayang performance or a Batik workshop is more than just an event; it is a sacred thread in the tapestry of our identity.

Our mission is to curate and digitize these experiences without stripping them of their soul. We provide the infrastructure for cultural preservation, making it effortless for the world to discover, book, and participate in the legacy of Surakarta.
