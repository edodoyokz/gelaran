Minimum Standards for Our Partners

To maintain the prestige of the Gelaran Cultural Archive, we require all organizers to meet specific criteria before approval.

  • check_circle
    Valid ID or Business Registration

    Organizers must be legally recognized entities or verified individuals.

  • check_circle
    Cultural Heritage Alignment

    Events must contribute to the preservation or evolution of cultural discourse.

  • check_circle
    High-Resolution Event Imagery

    A minimum of 5 high-quality visual assets are required for each listing.
