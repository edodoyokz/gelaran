The Midnight Shadow: A Wayang Kulit Masterpiece

location_on Pendhapa Ageng, Keraton Surakarta schedule 20:00 - 02:00 WIB

About the Performance

Witness the ancient art of storytelling come to life under the guidance of Master Puppeteer (Dalang) Ki Purbo Asmoro. This special three-night engagement explores the epic 'Mahabharata' through the lens of Javanese philosophy, accompanied by the hauntingly beautiful sounds of a full Gamelan orchestra.

Expect a journey through light and shadow that transcends mere entertainment, touching on themes of duty, cosmic balance, and the human spirit. This is a rare opportunity to experience the 'Malam Selaras'—a traditional midnight performance in its most authentic setting.
